## Instructions

Place your 5 IEEE papers related to knee injuries and orthopedics in this folder.

## Finding IEEE Papers

1. Visit: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/
2. Search for: "knee injury" or "ACL reconstruction" or "meniscus repair"
3. Filter by: Open Access (if possible)
4. Download PDFs and place them here

## Alternative Sources

IEEE may have limited orthopedic content. Also try:
- PubMed Central (open access)
- bioRxiv preprints
- MDPI open access journals
- Any open access medical journals

Just ensure papers are in PDF format.
